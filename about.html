About LET'S SCORE

LET'S SCORE is an educational platform dedicated to helping students learn Science and Physics in a simple and effective way.

We provide Notes, NCERT Solutions, MCQs, PYQs, Formula Sheets and Mock Tests for students.

Our Mission

To provide quality educational resources for Assamese and English medium students at affordable cost.

Subjects Covered

  • Science (Class VI – X)
  • Physics (Class XI – XII)
  • Assamese Medium
  • English Medium
